Tournament Guide

Scoring Rules

Welcome to the league! Here is exactly how scoring will work for the tournament. Read carefully so you don't lose points!

THE BASE SCORING (Every Match)

  • Correct Winner (W/D/L): +2 pts
  • Exact Scoreline: +3 pts (Max base score is +5 per match!)
  • Wrong Winner: -1 pt
  • No Prediction: -2 pts (DO NOT forget to make your picks! Ghosting the app will tank your score.)

KNOCKOUT STAGE RULES

  • Scores apply to the full match (90 mins + Extra Time).
  • If you predict a DRAW, you MUST also pick the Penalty Winner. You get your +2 points based on correctly guessing the team that advances.

THE GOLDEN BOOT BOOST (Knockouts Only)

  • Every player gets 5 Boosts to use during the Knockout Stage.
  • You can use a max of 1 boost per match.
  • How it works: If you use a boost and get the Correct Winner, you get a FLAT +3 BONUS POINTS.

THE UNDERDOG BONUS (New!)

  • Fortune favors the brave. If you correctly predict a massive upset (a heavy underdog winning, determined by the admin), you will get an automatic +2 BONUS POINTS.
  • This means a perfect underdog prediction with a Golden Boot can net you up to +10 points in a single match!

Good luck, set your alarms, and don't forget to submit your picks!
